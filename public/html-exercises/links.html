My Top Picks

My Top 3 Movies

Third in my list of top movies is Spirited Away. This is my first Studio Ghibli movie. I like this movie because it contains a lot of moral lessons and everyone can enjoy this movie. There’s also a little love team that makes me hope for a sequel.

The second spot in my list is Wolf Children. This one really moved me in an unexpected way. Although i could not relate to the main character, her attitude towards certain circumstances in her life inspired me to be strong. It also has a wonderful soundtrack that matches the mood of every scene. The art is also very beautiful. This was one of the movies that makes me ugly cry. So beautiful! I definitely recommend this movie.

My top movie is The Sound of Music. This one is a really special movie and will always be my number one because aside from the classic Disney movies, i consider this as my first favorite movie. I decided this when i was in grade 3. This is also my first musical film. After so many years, i can still sing along to the soundtrack with gusto.

My Top 3 Tunes

Third in my list is Best Friend (Acoustic) by Amy Winehouse. I just love how the acoustic guitar sets an intimate vibe in the song. I intend to learn how to play the song on the guitar as soon as i can. Nothing really special but songs that can motivate me to learn how to play it is a special thing and is ought to have a spot in this list.

The second in my list is Tooi Sakebi by Reichi Nakaido. I’ve been listening to blues lately, and while i was searching for an anime to binge watch, i came across Serial Experiments Lain and heard this song at the end of the anime. One listen and i was captivated immediately. I just like any music that sounds good (to me) apparently. So easy to please(hahaha). I like the “sad song to listen to while drinking beer in a pub” vibe of this song.

Last and definitely not the least would be Yellow by Coldplay. This is my most favorite of all ever since high school, and i still get chills when i listen to this especially to the live versions. i love the intensity of the lyrics and its meaning: doing anything for the person that you love even if you bleed from it, feel pain, encounter hurdles just for that special person. I could say that this would be my love song to that special person in my life who i have yet to meet in the future..If i ever went to a Coldplay concert and they play this, i’ll definitely cry.
